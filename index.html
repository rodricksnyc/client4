Bio

Naomi Cruz grew up in New York City and has worked in the entertainment industry throughout her whole life. She started modeling at the young age of 21 and was featured in multiple magazines, including segments for Maxim and FHM, New York Magazine, Copawear, A Magazine, Mass Appeal Magazine, and segments for Rolowear on Despirta America - Univision. Ms. Cruz has always had a huge interest in the beauty and entertainment industry and has worked as a professional makeup artist, during and after her modeling days.

Naomi has since transitioned into the field of marketing, after pursuing a bachelor’s degree from an accredited school in Manhattan in 2014. She continues to work freelance as a makeup artist in her spare time and is now pursuing a career in television and film. She has already made many appearances on prominent television shows in a very short amount of time, and would like to continue to make new connections and friends in the entertainment world. Naomi currently lives in Brooklyn, NY with her fiancé and her pomeranian, Leya.
